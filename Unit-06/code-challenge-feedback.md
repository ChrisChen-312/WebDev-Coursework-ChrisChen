Part 3: What I Learned & Changes I Made
From the client’s feedback, I noticed they wanted slightly smaller images and easier-to-read text. I also saw that they enjoyed the clean, calm design.
I made these improvements:
Reduced the image width to make the page more balanced.
Increased the font size slightly for better readability.
Kept the soft ocean colors because the client liked the calm feeling.
Maintained the clear structure of headings and lists.
