# Unit 7 Reflection
In Unit 7, I learned how to connect web design to real-world purpose, especially through the United Nations Global Goals. I explored how websites can support causes like ocean protection and community building, turning simple code into something meaningful.
I also reviewed the design thinking process, applying it to my community website project. From understanding the audience to iterating on my design, I saw how planning helps solve problems, not just build pages.
This unit changed how I see web design: it’s not just about pretty layouts, but about serving a purpose and making an impact. I’m excited to use these ideas in my future projects, creating websites that are both fun and useful.
