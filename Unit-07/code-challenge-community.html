About Our Community

Our Ocean Community is a group of people who love marine life and want to protect our oceans. We learn, share, and work together to keep our seas clean and safe for all creatures.

What We Do

  • Learn about marine animals
  • Share ocean facts
  • Join beach clean-up events
  • Protect sea creatures and their homes
