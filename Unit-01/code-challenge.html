🐙 The Differences Between Octopus, Squid, and Cuttlefish

1. Octopus

Class: Cephalopoda

Arms: 8 arms, no fins on head

Octopuses are soft-bodied and live alone.

They have no internal shell and are very smart.


2. Squid

Arms: 8 arms + 2 long tentacles

Squid have a soft internal shell called a “pen”.

They swim in groups and have two fins on their head.


3. Cuttlefish

Special feature: W-shaped pupils

They have a hard internal shell for buoyancy.

Not a fish They are cephalopods!


All three are amazing sea animals!
