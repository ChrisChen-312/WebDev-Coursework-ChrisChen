Discover Octopuses, Squids, and Cuttlefish

About My Client

My client is a marine life educator who loves teaching kids and students about ocean animals. They want a simple, clear web page to share facts about cephalopods.


Octopus

Key feature: 8 long arms

Octopuses are very intelligent and can solve problems. I included this because the client wants to highlight smart ocean animals.


Squid

Key feature: 8 arms + 2 tentacles

Squid swim fast and live in groups. This shows the diversity of sea life that the client cares about.


Cuttlefish

Fun fact: W-shaped pupils

Cuttlefish are not fish they are cephalopods! This fact is interesting for the client’s young audience.
